## Definition
> A precise mathematical notion of what it means to do the right thing in any particular circumstance.

## Intuition
Rather than imitating humans (who are not always rational), define "the right thing" formally and build systems whose computation achieves or approximates it. This reframes AI's goal from human-likeness to optimality of behaviour.

## How It Works
"The right thing" is that which is expected to maximise goal achievement, given the available information (see [[Rational Agent]]). AI then studies how to achieve such intelligent behaviour through computational means.

## Variants & Depth
> Underpins two of the four approaches to AI: "thinking rationally" (the laws-of-thought approach, via logic) and "acting rationally" (the rational-agent approach). Contrast with the human-centred [[Turing Test]] approach.
