## Definition
> One level of a game tree — a single move by one player. 2-ply = one move each for me and my opponent; 2n-ply = n moves each.

## Context
> Used in AI Lecture 03 to put a depth bound on minimax search (e.g. Deep Blue typically searched 14-ply).
