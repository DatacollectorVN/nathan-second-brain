## Definition
> A game-tree node representing a random event (dice roll, card deal, coin toss) rather than a player's choice; its value is the probability-weighted average of its children.

## Context
> AI Lecture 03: required for stochastic games like backgammon; handled by expectiminimax's Σ_r P(r)·ExpectMinimax(Result(s, r)) case.
