## Definition
**PRM800K** — an open dataset of **800,000 step-level human feedback labels** across **75K solutions** to **12K** [[MATH]] problems, released by OpenAI to train process-supervised reward models ([[PRM]]).

## Context
Introduced in [[Let's Verify Step by Step]] (2023). Each step is labeled positive/negative/neutral. Notably, ~4.5K MATH *test* problems are folded into the training split, so the paper evaluates on the remaining 500 test problems.
