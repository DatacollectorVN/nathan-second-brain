## Definition
**First-Correct Solutions** — a CoT pruning method by Chen et al. (2025) that linearly searches for the shortest CoT prefix that yields the correct final answer. Uses O(n) search.

## Context
Baseline that the binary cutting approach in [[Efficient Long CoT Reasoning in Small Language Models]] Improves upon (O(log n) instead of O(n)) and pairs with on-policy validation for SLM-aware pruning.
