## Definition
**Chain-of-Thought** — a prompting/training technique where a model produces intermediate reasoning steps before giving a final answer.

## Context
Originated as a prompting trick ("let's think step by step") in 2022. Now embedded into modern reasoning models via training, with reasoning wrapped in `<think>...</think>` tags.
