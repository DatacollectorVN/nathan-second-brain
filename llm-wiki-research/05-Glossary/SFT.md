## Definition
**Supervised Fine-Tuning** — training a pre-trained language model on labeled (input, output) pairs via cross-entropy loss. The standard first step of any fine-tuning pipeline.

## Context
Used to teach a model new behaviors, formats, or domains. Comes before preference-based methods like [[DPO]] or RLHF.
