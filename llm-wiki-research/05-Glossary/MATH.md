## Definition
**MATH benchmark** — 12,500 high-school competition-level math problems (Hendrycks et al., 2021). Standard medium-difficulty math reasoning eval. Most papers use the MATH-500 subset.

## Context
Significantly harder than [[GSM8K]] — strong SLMs score 50-65%, frontier models 80-90%.
