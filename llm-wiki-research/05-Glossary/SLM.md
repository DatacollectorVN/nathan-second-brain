## Definition
**Small Language Model** — a language model in the 1B–13B parameter range, designed for efficiency and on-device deployment. The 7B-class (Llama-3-8B, Qwen-2.5-7B, Mistral-7B) is the de facto research standard.

## Context
Contrast with frontier models (100B+) like GPT-4, Claude Opus, DeepSeek-R1. SLMs are the "deployable" tier.
