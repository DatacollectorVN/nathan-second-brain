## Definition
**WebShop** (Yao et al., 2022) — an online-shopping web environment with **1.18M real products** and **12k human instructions**, where an agent must navigate (search, choose options, buy) to purchase a product matching a natural-language instruction. Evaluated by average **score** and **success rate**.

## Context
A noisy real-world decision-making benchmark in [[ReAct - Synergizing Reasoning and Acting in Language Models|ReAct]], tested on **500** instructions. ReAct scores 66.6 / 40.0% SR — an absolute +10% SR over IL/IL+RL baselines, still well below human experts (82.1 / 59.6%).
